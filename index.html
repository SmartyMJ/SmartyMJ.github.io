Bio

Malik Jabati is a freshman at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill.
Only 18 years old, he's accomplished a lot in his short time on this earth.
In 2015 alone, Malik was named a U.S. Presidential Scholar, a Morehead-Cain
Scholar, and a National AP Scholar. He has worked in over eight countries and speaks
English, Spanish, French, and a bit of Arabic. He was a two-time Latin National Scholar
in high school, and is currently studying Brazilian Portuguese. In his free time, Malik cools off
by playing some tunes on his alto sax or shooting hoops in the basketball gym.
Always striving for perfection, he received a 36 out of 36 on the ACT and maintains
a perfect 4.0 GPA.

Right now, Malik is a volunteer teacher for Musical Empowerment, Outreach Chair
for the Black Student Movement First-Year Class Council, Event Coordinator for
UNC Young Democrats, Data Analyst for the Bradley Opere Student Body President
Campaign, and Academic Chair for the Alpha of North Carolina in the Sigma Phi Society.

Bachelor of Science: Computer Science
Minors: Business and Politics, Philosophy & Economics
